About me

I express compassion towards those who need help and I try to help them to the best of my ability, even if it sacrifices some of my own resources, such as time. I try my best to cooperate with others as well if we are working as a team. I will listen to the ideas and opinions of every team member without judgement and I will respect the team's final decision. I will avoid fighting with others at all costs so that no one is hurt.

Some online games that I enjoy playing


I enjoy listening to music to relieve my stress while doing work. I listen to many genres of music, with some of my favourites being pop music and Electronic Dance Music (EDM). I also love playing video games with my friends as it can entertain us for hours on end. My emotions tend to control me, leading to me making decisions based on what mood I am feeling at that point in time.
