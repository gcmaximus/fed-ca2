My experience before joining SP

I was first enrolled into Woodgrove Primary School (WGPS), a neighbourhood primary school. It was a lovely school with kind and caring teachers, who wanted the best for all students. My classmates were also very nice towards one another, never disrupting classes or causing a racket during lessons. Overall, I had fun learning in WGPS for my 6 years there because the learning environment is conducive and I rarely had trouble during my learning process.


After primary school, I transferred to St. Joseph's Institution (SJI). I instantly fell in love with the school as the community there is very warm and welcoming, and has a distinct "kampung spirit" as students and teachers bond and help out one another and spend much time together. Being in such a nice and friendly environment for 4 years, I enjoyed my time in SJI as I have learnt to become more caring and helpful towards others.
