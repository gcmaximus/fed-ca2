My experience in SP and School of Computing

Having studying in SP for approximately 2 months so far, I am enjoying my time here. The transition from secondary school to Singapore Polytechnic is a huge one as many aspects of education have changed, such as the education style. In secondary school, teachers would constantly make sure every student is studying well and doing their work properly during their school life.


Previous Next

On the other hand, in SP, there is a lot of independent learning to do, which is what I love about SP. I am given the freedom to adjust how I study instead of having to stick to a strict schedule. With my learning being controlled by my very own discipline, I will be motivated to study consistently. Additionally, I am also having fun studying in the School of Computing as all the modules that I have learnt so far have peaked my interest. With the motivation to learn more about the modules, it is easy for me to keep up with class work as I love what I am currently studying.
